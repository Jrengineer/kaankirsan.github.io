About Me

I'm Kaan Kırşan, an Electrical-Electronics Engineer from Eskişehir Osmangazi University with 5+ years of progressive experience at Doka Mühendislik. My career spans industrial automation, robotics, computer vision, and energy storage systems, with a proven track record in both technical engineering and project management.

My technical expertise centers on motor control systems and energy storage solutions, combined with comprehensive project management capabilities. I'm passionate about solving complex engineering challenges through innovative automation, delivering measurable business value, and continuously expanding my technical expertise.
